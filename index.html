About

10+ years

Based in Toronto, permitted to work with IATSE 873. Over a decade spent chasing clean dialogue on location, no matter what type of challenges a shoot offers.

I started my career in sound on the reality show Survivor. Beginning as challenge tester, I shadowed the audio team and got the opportunity to chase audio across the jungles of Cambodia and the islands of Fiji. That early trial-by-fire shaped how I work — fast, adaptable, and calm when the conditions aren't.

Since then I've mixed sound across movies, documentaries, music videos, reality & competition shows, commercials, short films, PSAs, and social content. Time spent in other departments along the way gave me a real understanding of what makes a set run smooth.

Technically fluent in wireless radio systems, confident on the boom, and precise with lav placement, I bring a kit built for speed and a temperament built for problem-solving under pressure.
